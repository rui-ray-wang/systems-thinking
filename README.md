# Systems Thinking — Interactive Learning Tools

Interactive web tools for Session 4 of **AI for Strategic Decision-Making & Innovation**.
No installation required — runs entirely in the browser.

## Course Context

These tools accompany the in-class activities for Session 4 of **AI for Strategic Decision-Making & Innovation**, which apply systems thinking to real cases including Nokia's decline, Apple's ecosystem, and Beijing's traffic policy.

## Tools

### 1. Visual Concept Explorer
**File:** `systems-thinking-animations.html`

Ten animated diagrams that visualize core systems thinking concepts in motion:

| # | Concept | What the animation shows |
|---|---------|--------------------------|
| 1 | Stocks | A bathtub filling and draining with adjustable inflow/outflow |
| 2 | Flows | Water molecules moving between reservoirs |
| 3 | Reinforcing Loops | Compound interest accelerating over time |
| 4 | Balancing Loops | A thermostat correcting toward a target temperature |
| 5 | Delays | Short delay (thermostat) vs. long delay (Nokia's collapse) — 3 scenes |
| 6 | Tipping Points | A system resisting change until a threshold is crossed |
| 7 | Fixes That Fail | A symptomatic fix that worsens the underlying problem |
| 8 | Shifting the Burden | Dependency forming as fundamental solutions are neglected |
| 9 | Limits to Growth | Rabbit population hitting carrying capacity; startup growth hitting a constraint |
| 10 | Leverage Points | Meadows' intervention levels from parameters to paradigms |

### 2. Concept Relationship Map
**File:** `systems-thinking-concept-map.html`

A draggable, interactive map of 16 interconnected systems thinking concepts.

- **Drag nodes** to arrange the map to match your mental model
- **Mark knowledge level** per node: Know / Fuzzy / Unknown
- **Auto-layout** button applies force-directed spring simulation
- **Filter presets**: Full Map, Feedback Loops, Archetypes, Leverage Points
- **Generate AI study prompt**: copies a personalized prompt based on your knowledge markings — paste into Claude or ChatGPT for targeted explanations
